Harry Styles

Get ready to learn way too much about my favorite singer!

Born February 1st, 1994, 28 year old British singer Harry Styles started his career in 2010 in the boy band One Direction. Following their breakup in 2015, Styles started his solo career with the launch of his self-titled album, and his fan base was hooked. He has since released two more albums and accomplished things his 16 year old self never thought would be possible. Click on the tabs to read more!
