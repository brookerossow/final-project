Harry Styles: Early Life

Where it all began... Harry auditioned for The X Factor in 2010 singing Stevie Wonder's "Isn't She Lovely"

The five boys above (from left: Harry Styles, Niall Horan, Zayn Malik, Louis Tomlinson, and Liam Payne) all thought they didn't make it to the next round of auditions, but instead Simon Cowell put them into a boy band, One Direction.

The boys started performing together, and their fan base grew rapidly.

The boys dropped their first album, "Up All Night," in 2011. Soon after, they started the Up All Night Tour, going from 2011-2012.

They then dropped their second album, "Take Me Home," in 2012. Once again, they started the Take Me Home Tour in 2013.

They then dropped the "Midnight Memories" album in 2013, getting back on their tour bus to start the Where We Are Tour in 2014.

Harry's designated mic color while in One Direction was green.

The boys then dropped their fourth studio album, "Four," and embarked on the On The Road Again tour in 2015.

This was Harry's first time wearing Gucci out, at the 2015 American Music Awards, starting a long partnership with the brand.

Before splitting, One Direction released one last album named "Made In The A.M.," without ex-member Zayn Malik. They never toured this album.
